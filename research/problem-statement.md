# Problem Statement

## The Problem

Modern research is increasingly difficult to manage.

Researchers conducting systematic reviews often rely on multiple disconnected tools to search, organize, screen, extract, synthesize, and report scientific evidence.

A typical workflow may involve:

- Scopus or Web of Science for searching
- Zotero for references
- Rayyan for screening
- Excel for extraction
- ChatGPT for summaries
- Word for writing
- PRISMA diagrams created manually

This fragmented workflow leads to:

- Repetitive manual work
- Loss of traceability
- Inconsistent documentation
- Limited reproducibility
- Expensive software subscriptions
- Poor collaboration
- AI outputs without transparent evidence

---

## Why This Matters

Scientific literature is growing faster than researchers can reasonably read and synthesize.

Students, researchers, NGOs, government agencies, and consultants spend significant time organizing information rather than generating knowledge.

Existing tools solve individual tasks but rarely support the complete evidence synthesis workflow in an open, transparent, and reproducible manner.

---

## Opportunity

Evident aims to become an open-source Research Intelligence Platform that integrates literature management, evidence synthesis, AI-assisted reasoning, and reproducible research workflows into one cohesive system.

Rather than replacing researchers, Evident assists them by reducing repetitive tasks while preserving scientific rigor, transparency, and evidence traceability.

---

## Guiding Principle

Every AI-generated insight must be supported by verifiable evidence from the uploaded literature collection.

Evidence always comes before opinion.
